Angles
(5 hours)

Identify alternate and corresponding angles, and know that they are equal.
Understand a proof that angles in a triangle/quadrilateral sum to 180/360?.
Solve a range of geometrical problems using side/angle properties of triangles/quadrilaterals.

Explain how to find, calculate and use the sums of interior/exterior angles of quadrilaterals, pentagons, and hexagons, and the interior/exterior of regular polygons.
