Stage 2

Number

Solve addition and subtraction problems using numbers with up to 2 decimal places in
the context of measurement or money, without the use of a calculator.
Solve multiplication & division problems involving multiplication up to a two-digit
number by a one-digit number, without the use of a calculator.
Solve division problems, interpreting the result.
Convert ½ and ¼ to and from percentage form and calculate 25%, 50% of simple
quantities, including money; read and estimate percentages from percentage scales and
scaled pie charts.
Work out starting times, finishing times and intervals without the use of a calculator.

 
Algebra Recognise, describe, continue and explain patterns in number.
Use word formulae in context; substitute positive integers into the formula to find the
value of the subject.

 
Shape Estimate lengths and angles by comparison.
Use kilograms and grams and convert measurements from one unit to another.
Measure and draw angles to the nearest degree; distinguish between acute, obtuse, reflex
and right angles.
Recognise simple solids and their nets.
Recognise and complete reflection symmetry of 2-D and 3-D shapes.
Use and interpret street plans (including simple grid references, left and right, clockwise
and anticlockwise, and compass directions).		  
Data

Understand and use the probability scale.
Find the mode and median value of a small set of discrete data.
Extract and use information from common two-way tables.
