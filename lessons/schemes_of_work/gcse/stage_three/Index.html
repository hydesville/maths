Stage 3

Number

Order positive and negative temperatures; solve problems involving temperature
changes.
Multiply and divide numbers with no more than one decimal digit by an integer between
1 and 10 without the use of a calculator.
Multiply and divide any number (with up to 2 decimal places) by powers of ten without
the use of a calculator.
Calculate a fraction of a given quantity.
Calculate simple percentages (10%, 20%, 30%, 5%, 15%) of quantities without the use
of a calculator.
Identify fractions; recall the fraction to decimal conversions of familiar simple fractions.
Perform calculations involving the use of brackets and the hierarchy of operations.


Algebra Solve simple equations involving only one operation.
In context, use formulae expressed in words or symbols; substitute positive numbers into
the formula to find the value of the subject.
Construct and interpret simple graphs, including conversion graphs.		  
Shape Know rough metric equivalents of pounds, feet and gallons.
Make sensible estimates of a range of measures in everyday settings.
Use litres and millilitres and convert measurements from one unit to another; interpret
scales on a range of measuring instruments.
Calculate the area of rectangles and right-angled triangles.
Use 2-D representations of 3-D shapes.
Construct and interpret scale drawings using simple scale factors.		  
Data

Understand and use measures of probability from equally likely outcomes.
Calculate the mean and the range of discrete data.
Draw and interpret simple frequency tables, charts and bar charts for discrete data.
